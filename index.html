Indian spice masala, commonly referred to as "masala," is an integral part of Indian cuisine and culture. It is a blend of various ground spices and herbs used to enhance the flavor, aroma, and color of a wide range of dishes. Masalas are used in both vegetarian and non-vegetarian cooking, and they vary in composition based on regional preferences and the type of dish being prepared.
